The Mandate

Our Mandate for ministry was received from the LORD Himself, in an 18 hour vision. During this vision, a commission was received from the LORD to liberate mankind in all facets of human existence, to restore broken destinies, to bring healing to the infirmed. This was the Divine mandate received from the LORD: Now the hour has come to liberate the world from all oppression of the devil through the preaching of the word of faith, and I am sending you to undertake this task

Today, testimonies of liberation through our messages, books, tapes, magazines and other periodicals are most humbling. The word of faith is working like fire for the liberation of mankind across the nations.
